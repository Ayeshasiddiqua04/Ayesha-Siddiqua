What is Robotics?

Robotics is a branch of engineering and science that deals with the design, construction, operation, and use of robots. It combines various fields such as mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science.

Applications of Robotics

  • Manufacturing and Assembly
  • Healthcare (surgery, rehabilitation)
  • Space Exploration
  • Military and Defense
  • Home Automation

Future of Robotics

The future of robotics is promising, with advancements in AI and machine learning paving the way for more autonomous and intelligent systems. We can expect robots to play a larger role in daily life, enhancing efficiency and safety.
